  • RustyJames
    "Like a crack of thunder on an otherwise calm day, Jason Molina's sparse, haunting and extremely personal project, Songs: Ohia, is meant to haunt and unnerve. His music exemplifies the power of one man and an amplified guitar, which is all he gives you to latch on to, 90% of the time. A gifted singer and songwriter, with a penetrative voice to match. Fits nicely alongside Will Oldham and Bill Callahan in the sad bastard sweepstakes."
    • RYM Rough Guide for Songs: Ohia
